China Buddies


China Buddies are German students in THL who volunteer to participate in a study buddy program with Chinese Students. when new generations of Chinese students arrive in Luebeck, China Buddies help them to find their way on the campus and to introduce some shops, supermarkets, restaurants, public transportation to them. In addition, during the free time, China Buddies and Chinese students would like to organize some international activities like parties and travelling. This program can be a good starting point that Chinese students begin to make friends with German students and to be familiar with the life in Germany.

Activities

Barbecue Party

Chinese students and their Chinabuddies came together to the Barbecue party on June 7th. This party was held by China Coordination Office and was held at the garden of Building 1. Students made the barbecue themselves, as well as other food they made at home. The party was permeated with an atmosphere of friendship. Every one is happy.


7.6.2018
by LIU Yanran

Birthday Party

Sarah and Shi xinyue celebrated their birthday together at the kitchen of ground floor in the dormitory. We prepared for the party for a long time. We bought a lot of foods and drinks, as well as some decorations. It is fortunately to have the same birthday with others. Both Sarah and Shi Xinyue were very happy.



15.4.2018
by LIU Yanran

Traveling in Molfsee

We went to an open-air museum in Kiel called Molfsee on April 7th. The weather was very good that day. We took a lot of photos together. And there are many different kind of animals. It is a good place to relax. Students were interested in this open-air museum and enjoyed the trip.



7.4.2018
by LIU Yanran

City Tour in Luebeck

As soon as Chinese students of this generation arrived in Luebeck, China Coordination Office organized a city tour in Luebeck. During this tour, we had the first impression of this beautiful city. And this is the first time we traveled with our new German friends. We took a group photo in front of the Holstein Tor.



9.3.2018
by LIU Yanran
