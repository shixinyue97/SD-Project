Malta has been inhabited since approximately 5900 BC. Its location in the centre of the Mediterranean has historically given it great strategic importance as a naval base. Most of these foreign influences have left some sort of mark on the country's ancient culture.The picture on the right side was taken from Dingli Cliff. The sunset was beautifully recorded in lens. Malta is worth visiting, here you can experience a completely different atmosphere from that of Germany. The hot weather here is extremely suitable for vacation. And the beaches here provide you the best place for swimming and diving.

by Kong Yu




Hamburg is a beautiful city in north Germany. I have visited Hamburg many times but it's my first time to be here during Christmas. Luckily I have seen the Christmas parade in the city center of Hamburg. During the parade, many children played different roles, such as Santa Claus and deer. It was so funny. After the parade, I tried some food in the Weilnachtenmarkt.Gluehwein tastes very delicious, it's the most famous speciality in Germany. You won't miss it!

by Kong Yu




We went to Oslo and Bergen during Christmas. The highlights of our trip were the delicious food and beautiful landscape. We took the train from Bergen to Oslo, it took us about seven hours to finish this trip. Although it may sound a little bit boring, but the snow-covered mountains were breathtaking. After we arrived in Oslo, we tried the local sea food restaurants. The picture is a dish called "sea food platter". There are fish, shrimp, mussel, crab inside. We have also visited the museums in Oslo. Oslo was not so cold as I had imagined. The trip was pleasant!

by Kong Yu
